# E-Commerce Funnel Optimization & Retention Strategy

## Problem
High traffic but low conversion due to drop-offs in the customer journey.

## Approach
- Conducted funnel analysis to identify drop-off points
- Applied RFM segmentation to identify high-value users
- Built dashboard for conversion tracking

## Results
- Identified Product Detail Page (PDP) as main bottleneck
- Improved visibility of key conversion metrics

## Key Insight
User activity peaks at specific hours, creating opportunities for targeted campaigns.

## Business Impact
Improved conversion efficiency and increased customer lifetime value through retention strategies.
