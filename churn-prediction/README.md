# Customer Churn Prediction

## Problem
High-value customers churn at a higher rate, leading to significant revenue loss.

## Approach
- Built predictive model to identify churn risk early
- Focused on high Recall to detect potential churners
- Used customer behavior analysis for feature insights

## Results
- Achieved 90% Recall and 0.95 AUC
- Identified early churn signals within first 6 months

## Key Insight
Customers using more services have significantly lower churn risk (~50% reduction).

## Business Impact
Estimated revenue recovery of $241K annually through targeted retention strategies.
